About Me

Recent graduate from the University of Washington, with a major in both Operations Management and Information Systems.

I am fascinated by technology and the capabilities it has to shape our world. At the same time, I am constantly seeking to better understand the people around me. My goal is to bridge the gap between these two worlds.

I've built a diverse skill-set to reach this goal. I am comfortable leading teams, and just as comfortable diving into the back-end of a jumbled code base. I love communicating face-to-face with clients, but I also value a day spent getting familiar with the latest Javascript library. A desire for continual learning is what drives me - I value curiosity, hard work, and discipline, and try to reflect these qualities in everything I do.

I am always looking for opportunities to challenge and grow my skills, both personally and professionally. If you are of a similar mindset, I'd love to connect.
